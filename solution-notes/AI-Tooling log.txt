Following is the log of AI tooling in terms of how AI assistance was utilized and how its suggestions were accepted/rejected.

1. Initially, EmbeddingPolicySearchService only returned chunks with a similarity score above 0.70. During testing, 
I found that relevant chunks were being excluded due to wording differences. After exploring retrieval strategies with AI and 
validating the results myself, I removed the fixed threshold to improve retrieval quality.

2. Another reason for less scores from ConsineSimilarity was the size of chunks I was creating. I was initially creating chunks
based on the section (containing all the sub sections in it). Which made the chunk text quite large and the embeddings were 
not accurately being compared with the question's embedding. As a result, relevant embeddings were ignored, score went down and 
wrong answers/citations were returned in response. I used AI to refine this approach and instead chunked the policy document based on section, 
sub section which improved the relevance of retrieved context.

3. There were some issues with react project's copilotkit/react-core/v2/style.css reference. Due to which the default built-in chat
screen was not populating. I used AI to understand what the problem was and eventually removed 

4. There were issues with the React project's @copilotkit/react-core/v2/styles.css import, 
which prevented the built-in chat UI from rendering correctly. I used AI to investigate the issue and eventually removed the 
problematic dependency/configuration and adjusted the frontend setup so the application could communicate correctly with the MAF AG-UI backend.

5. I used AI to diagnose CopilotKit streaming failures with Ollama due to which I was getting connection errors in the frontend app. 
Some guide on the internet suggested I should align the versions of the model and also use a middleware which injects a messageId 
into streamed responses.
